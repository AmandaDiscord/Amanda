Amanda is a 100% free / command music bot written in TypeScript, compiled for the NodeJS runtime.
Developed by the AmandaDiscord org, Amanda aims to be free, dependable, and cute - in no particular order.
The software we integrate with is mostly proprietary, but we'd like to thank the following individuals/libraries:
DasWolke, discord.js, and all of Amanda's donors ❤️
